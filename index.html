Point of View

In the AI age, PMs work with something that doesn't just execute, it anticipates. The gap between idea and execution has shrunk so much that good PMs have been set free.

AI automates the craft. What remains is the art - designing for trust, understanding emotion, deciding what deserves to exist.

The best PMs of this era won't only optimize funnels. They'll translate between intelligence and intent. Speed isn't progress. Progress is knowing where you're going - and why.
